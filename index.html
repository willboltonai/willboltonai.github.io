Module: Understanding Artificial Intelligence August 2023

Learning Outcomes:

1. Develop an understanding of the history and future of Artificial Intelligence. To include definition of AI, professional and ethical issues, as well as key application areas (Industry 4.0 and FinTech).
2. Develop an understanding of AI fundamentals including the Alan Turing Test, knowledge representation and approaches to developing learning systems.
3. Examine programming paradigms and algorithms for Machine Learning.
4. Examine the preparation data for AI solutions.
5. Develop programming abilities for learning algorithm implementation.

On completion of this module I am looking foward to understanding the professional and ethical issues brought about by using AI in various settings. I hope to understand better the impact of AI on society. I aim to be able to critically analyse the essential concepts in machine learning approaches, implementing AI solutions, and the underlying programming paradigms for AI technologies.

On completion of this module, I am looking forward to understanding the key mathematical and statistical concepts and techniques that underpin mechanisms in Data Science and AI.
